Venture People

Established July 2000

Venture People Corp. is one of the top three leading local executive search firms in Korea servicing clients from local conglomerate companies to foreign MNC’s in Korea as well as those in overseas covering most of the prominent industries with 60 professional consultants and researchers from three different office locations in Seoul, Korea.

We are also a member firm of NPA(Venture People Corp. is the only member in Korea since year 2005), a global executive search association with global presence and memberships well over 500 member firms in 60 different countries in the world with 4,500 professional consultants from North America, South America, Europe, Middle East, Africa, Australia, New Zealand and Asia, all data and information are being shared among members to deliver most efficient search results for any global assignments for clients in all levels.

We are also an official affiliate and partner search firm of KAIST the Graduate School of Management, Yonsei University Alumni Association and KOTRA through Invest Korea and have been rendering our service since year 2000 and awarded as the ‘Best Recruiting and Employment Service Firm’ by the Ministry of Labor in year 2008. And since then, Venture People has retained its award and awarded consecutively for past 12 years.
